Pose

Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

Pose is a real-time photo coaching app. This policy explains what information the app handles and — mostly — what it deliberately does not.

The short version

Your camera feed, your photos, and your body-pose data never leave your device. Pose has no account system, no analytics of your images, and no server that receives them.

Camera and photos

Pose uses the camera to detect your body position and coach you in real time. All pose detection runs on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Video frames are processed in memory and are not stored, uploaded, or shared. Photos you choose to capture are saved directly to your photo library and are accessible only to you.

Subscriptions

Pose offers auto-renewable subscriptions. Payment is processed entirely by Apple through your App Store account; we never see your payment details. Subscription state (whether your subscription is active) is managed through Apple and our paywall infrastructure provider, Superwall, which processes anonymous device identifiers and subscription events to present the paywall and honor your purchase. See Superwall's privacy policy.

What we do not collect

  • No name, email, or account — the app has no sign-up.
  • No photos or camera frames — processing is on-device only.
  • No location data.
  • No advertising identifiers and no ad networks.

Children

Pose is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly process personal information from them.
